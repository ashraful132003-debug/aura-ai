Clinical Ethics

Our Trust, Boundaries & Safety Protocols

Aura is a mental wellness companion, not a replacement for clinical therapy. We maintain clear boundaries to ensure safety and redirect users in crisis immediately to real-world help.

✅ What Aura CAN do

  • Provide comforting, empathetic active listening.
  • Offer evidence-based relaxation and breathing paced guides.
  • Teach lifestyle management, sleep guidelines, and organization.
  • Offer completely private local tracking logs.

❌ What Aura CANNOT do

  • Provide medical diagnosis or clinical medication advice.
  • Substitute professional treatment, psychologists, or clinics.
  • Answer unrelated general knowledge (GK) trivia queries.
  • Listen to or encourage harmful ideation without raising a crisis flag.

Our Story

Safety & Ethics

Clear boundaries.
Real care.

Aura is a mental wellness companion — not a therapist. We're transparent about what we can and can't do.

✅ What Aura can do

  • ✓ Offer empathetic, active listening and comfort
  • ✓ Guide evidence-based breathing and relaxation exercises
  • ✓ Suggest sleep hygiene, organisation, and lifestyle tips
  • ✓ Track your mood and show weekly wellness patterns
  • ✓ Provide fully private, local conversation logs

❌ What Aura cannot do

  • ✕ Provide medical diagnoses or prescribe medication
  • ✕ Replace professional therapy or psychiatric care
  • ✕ Answer general knowledge or unrelated queries
  • ✕ Guarantee outcomes or clinical results
  • ✕ Ignore signs of crisis — safety always comes first
